The Framework

Meet the agents.

LeClaw is an open-source RevOps agent framework. Each agent owns a specific operational domain — running proactively, fixing problems before your reps notice.

Le Data Quality
Scans your CRM for duplicate records, missing required fields, stale contacts, and broken data. Surfaces a fix list or auto-resolves — your call.
Live in Beta
Le Plumber
Monitors integration health across your GTM stack. Detects broken webhooks, failed syncs, and API errors before they become rep complaints.
Coming Soon
Le Territory Expert
Detects misrouted accounts, coverage gaps, and ownership conflicts. Keeps your territory model clean as your org scales.
Roadmap
Le Deal Ops
Automates Deal Desk workflows — routing approvals, flagging exceptions, and keeping quotes moving without manual intervention.
Roadmap
Le GTM Analytics
Automated funnel instrumentation and KPI monitoring. Know when numbers move before your CRO asks why.
Roadmap
Le Order Ops
Automates the operational layer between closed-won and revenue recognized. ARR tracking, order processing, and renewal hygiene.

How It Works

Connect. Configure. Done.

1
Connect your CRM
Authorize LeClaw against your Salesforce or HubSpot instance. Read-only by default — you control what agents can write.
2
Choose your agents
Select which agents run, on what schedule, and whether they auto-fix or surface a review list first.
3
RevOps runs itself
Agents run proactively, catch problems before your reps notice, and keep your GTM stack clean without manual intervention.
