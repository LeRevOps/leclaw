// architecture

Le Directeur
runs the town.

Every citizen files a report. Le Directeur reads them all and synthesizes one answer. Le Témoin reviews every write-back before it touches your CRM.

LD
Le Directeur
mayor's right hand · coordinates the town
The orchestrating agent. Coordinates which citizens run, in what order, with what context. Reads every report and synthesizes a final insight no individual citizen can see alone. Talk to it however you like — it knows what to do.
dispatches → citizens
12
the citizens
specialists · each files a report
Each citizen owns a domain: data quality, pipeline stage, BDR follow-up, forecast accuracy. They fetch only broken records from your CRM — no full scans, no wasted compute. Every citizen returns a structured report: a score, flagged issues, and a plain-English summary.
proposes → write-back
LT
Le Témoin
write-back queue · reviews before every change
No change touches your CRM without passing through Le Témoin. Approval can be automatic (high-confidence, low-risk) or require human sign-off. Write-back is always opt-in.
